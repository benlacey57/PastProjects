Founded in Coulsdon, Ideal Glass & Glazing has spent two decades bridging the gap between mass-market retailers and high-end architectural glazing. We don't just supply glass; we manage the entire lifecycle from laser-accurate templating to final structural sign-off.

Our commitment is simple: No contractors. Every glazier who enters your property is a full-time, directly employed member of our family team, trained to uphold the safety standards of BS EN 12600.
