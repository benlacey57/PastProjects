The Standard in Bespoke Glazing

"At Ideal Glass & Glazing, we don't just supply glass; we engineer light and space."

Founded in 2004, Ideal Glass & Glazing Ltd began with a simple mission: to provide the homeowners and businesses of Surrey with high-quality, bespoke glass products that high-street retailers simply couldn't offer. Based in our central workshop in Coulsdon, our reach extends across the entirety of Surrey and London.

Whether you are a homeowner looking to maximize natural light in a Victorian terrace or a commercial project manager overseeing a high-traffic retail fit-out, our requirements remain the same: precision, safety, and longevity. We specialize in high-performance glass solutions that meet the rigorous BS EN 12600 safety standards, ensuring that every installation—from a delicate antique mirror to a structural glass balustrade—is fit for purpose.

Our "Personal Touch" isn't just a marketing slogan; it defines our operational workflow. Unlike national glazing chains, we do not use third-party contractors. Every surveyor and installer is an IG&G employee, trained to handle high-value materials with the utmost care. This vertical integration allows us to maintain a 99% "First-Time Fit" rate, drastically reducing lead times.
