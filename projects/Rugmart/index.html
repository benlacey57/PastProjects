Before You Visit Rugmart

Measure Your Space

Take measurements of the area including length and width. Consider furniture placement—will the rug go under a dining table or in the living room?

Bring Swatches & Media

Carry fabric samples or paint swatches. Take pictures and Videos of your room from different angles to help visualize.

Style & Color

Think about traditional, modern, or minimalistic styles. Save inspiration photos on your phone for our experts.

Lifestyle & Usage

Pets or kids? Opt for stain-resistant wool. Hallways need durable materials; bedrooms need plush, soft textures.
