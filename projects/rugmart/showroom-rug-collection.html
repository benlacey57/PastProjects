Vintage Hall Runners

Our hand-picked vintage runners are sourced from the tribal regions of Persia and the Caucasus. These pieces are chosen specifically for their ability to withstand the high-traffic demands of modern Surrey hallways while providing an immediate sense of history and warmth.

Handmade Tribal Kilims

The Kilim is a flat-weave rug that offers a more minimalist profile than a knotted pile rug. Our vintage Kilims from the mid-20th century showcase bold geometric symbols and organic dyes that have softened beautifully over decades.

Small Vintage Accents

Versatile and vibrant, these smaller pieces (up to 1.5m) are perfect for studies, bedside placement, or even wall mounting. Each rug is a unique masterwork of nomadic art, featuring intricate borders and central medallions.

Medium Room-Size Rugs

Ideally sized for standard UK living rooms, our medium vintage rugs bring a sophisticated, lived-in feel to contemporary interiors. These rugs have already stood the test of time, proving their exceptional structural durability.

Oversized Mansion Rugs

Our rarest finds. These large-scale vintage rugs (3m x 4m and above) were once the centerpieces of grand estates. They offer unparalleled luxury and warmth for large open-plan spaces or formal dining rooms.
