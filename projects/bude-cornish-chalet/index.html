On Your Doorstep

Explore Beautiful Bude & Cornwall

The chalet sits minutes from world-class surf beaches and the famous South West Coast Path.

🏊

Bude Sea Pool

2 min walk

A magnificent tidal swimming pool set in the rocks, naturally refreshed by Atlantic waves. Open all year — free for all to use.

🏰

Bude Castle Heritage Centre

5 min walk

Discover the fascinating history of Bude and the Bude Canal in this charming free museum, housed in a Regency cliff-top castle.

🥾

South West Coast Path

From your doorstep

World-class coastal walking from your front door. Over 630 miles of breathtaking cliff-top trails with dramatic Atlantic views.

🗿

Tintagel Castle

45 min drive

The legendary birthplace of King Arthur perched on dramatic Atlantic cliffs. A truly awe-inspiring site with deep Cornish myth.

⚓

Boscastle & Port Isaac

35–45 min drive

Charming fishing villages with historic harbour walls. Port Isaac is famously Doc Martin country — utterly picture-perfect.

🌊

Bude Surf Schools

5 min walk to beach

Beginners to advanced — Bude's Atlantic beaches produce perfect waves. Multiple surf schools on Summerleaze and Crooklets beaches.
