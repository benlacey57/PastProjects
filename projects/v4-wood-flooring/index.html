Established 2002

Decades of Craftsmanship

V4 Wood Flooring is a family-run business based in the South of England. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide the finest quality hardwood floors while maintaining a commitment to environmental stewardship.

Every plank is a testament to our obsession with detail, from the selection of the raw timber to the hand-finished oils that bring the grain to life.
