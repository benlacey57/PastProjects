The DeLaBaie Difference

With premium percentages of top-rated actives and 95% natural ingredients, we ensure our products will really make a difference.

The Cloudberry

A flaming amber gold, the Cloudberry is the rarest and most elusive Arctic berry — the crème de la crème of Arctic oils. Richly endowed with antioxidants, Omega fatty acids, UV-protecting carotenoids and cell-membrane-strengthening phytosterols, it is known in the far north as the Royal Berry. It features in our most prestigious formulas.

Sea Buckthorn

Used for centuries in Arctic and Asian medicine, Sea Buckthorn Berry Oil is rich in palmitoleic acid (Omega-7) and packed with carotenoids, antioxidants and natural Vitamin E. This powerful orange oil has extraordinary skin-regenerating properties, counters harmful UV effects and has long been a traditional treatment for skin healing. We use both the berry and seed oil.

Arctic Birch Extract

Extracted from the white bark of Arctic Birch, this remarkable ingredient contains essential fatty acids, anti-inflammatory triterpenoids and betulinic acid. Rich in betulin and lupeol, it counteracts the damaging effects of UV light and has attracted significant attention from cancer scientists for its effect on melanoma cells. It is a cornerstone of our anti-ageing formulations.

CO2 Extraction

All our Arctic seed oils and plant extracts are CO2 extracted. Significant amounts of valuable bioactive compounds are lost in traditional steam extraction, but CO2 is a gentler and more effective method of preserving the natural plant bio-actives. Our pure extraction processes also ensure all extracts are free from solvents and heavy metal residues.

Paraben-Free Formulas

All our blended oils are preservative free, and our creams and lotions are paraben free. We use only effective broad-spectrum preservatives where necessary to inhibit bacterial, fungal and yeast growth. Our Squalane is always plant-derived from olive oil — never from threatened shark species — reflecting our commitment to responsible sourcing at every level.

Ethical Sourcing

Meticulous, ethical research into the true nature of ageing skin from trusted suppliers lies at the heart of the ingredients we use. Our Arctic Peat is collected only by registered suppliers committed to preserving the fragility of the eco-system. We believe beautiful skin should never come at the expense of the natural world that provides our finest ingredients.

Clinically Researched

We use premium percentages of top-rated actives backed by clinical research. Every ingredient we select has been chosen on the basis of its proven efficacy in treating the true nature of ageing skin. We don't use token amounts of active ingredients — we use levels at which research demonstrates measurable results you can see and feel.

Ancient Arctic Wisdom

A knowledge and skill of the very best of skincare gained from ancient Arctic wisdom underpins our entire formulation philosophy. Plants and berries that survive the extreme darkness and cold of the Arctic winter, then flourish in seventy-three days of continuous summer sunlight, develop a remarkable concentration of bioactive compounds that are scientifically proven to benefit human skin.

Hand-Crafted Quality

Every DeLaBaie product is lovingly hand-crafted and blended in small batches to ensure consistent quality and freshness of actives. By selling direct to you without intermediaries, we can invest that saving back into premium ingredient percentages rather than marketing margins — meaning you receive genuinely superior skincare at a price that reflects our values, not our overheads.
